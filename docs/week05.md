##### Why are you learning Hungarian? [A2-B1]
###### When I was a student in England, I spent a year in Budapest. The university had an exchange program. I learned a little Hungarian: "Where is the bus?" and "Two hot dogs please." Years later, I had a Hungarian friend at work. He asked me: "Why don’t you learn Hungarian again?" I love the sound of Hungarian. It’s almost as beautiful as French. It’s not hard, just different...
