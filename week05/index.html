    Miért tanultok magyarul?¶

    Why are you learning Hungarian?¶
    When I was a student in England, I spent a year in Budapest. The university had an exchange program. I learned a little Hungarian: "Where is the bus?" and "Two hot dogs please." Years later, I had a Hungarian friend at work. He asked me: "Why don’t you learn Hungarian again?" I love the sound of Hungarian. It’s almost as beautiful as French. It’s not hard, just different... (This is past tense, A2-B1 level)¶
    Magyarul Angolul Jegyzet
    Miért tanultok magyarul? Why are you learning Hungarian? Present tense, tanultok = you plural, tanulsz = you singular
    Amikor egyetemista voltam Angliában When I was a uni student in England amikor = when, voltam = I was, egyetemista = university, -ista = member/student; or amikor diák voltam = when I was a student
    Egy évet Budapesten töltöttem I spent a year in Budapest évet = year Obj, töltöttem = spent-I (past)
    Az egyetemnek volt csereprogramja The university had an exchange program egy-etem = one-institution (uni) -nek for, csere-program = exchange-program, -ja = its
    Kicsit tanultam magyarul I learned a little Hungarian tanul-tam = learn-I (past), -ul = in (language)
    "Hol van a busz?" "Where is the bus?"
    "Két virslit kérek" "Two hot dogs please" két two, virslit sausage Obj, kérek I request / I ask for
    Évek múlva Years later év-ek = years, múlva = after passing
    Volt egy magyar barátom a munkahelyen I had a Hungarian friend at work volt = was/had, barát-om = friend-my, munka-hely-en = work-place-in
    Ő kérdezte He asked kérdez-te = asked-he/she
    "Miért nem tanulsz újra magyarul?" "Why don't you learn Hungarian again?" tanul-sz = learn-you, újra = again
    Imádom a magyar hangzását I love the sound of Hungarian imádom = I love, hangzását = -a its sound -t Obj
    Majdnem olyan szép, mint a francia It’s almost as beautiful as French majdnem = almost, olyan ... mint = as ... as
    Nem nehéz, csak más It’s not hard, just different nem = not, nehéz = hard, csak = only/just, más = different
